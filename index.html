Official Publisher Profile

Nesia Books — Crafting Learning, One Page at a Time

Founded in 2014 in Chennai, Nesia Books has grown into a trusted educational publisher serving schools across Tamil Nadu and beyond. We specialize in curriculum-aligned books for early childhood through Grade 8.

Our editorial team, comprising experienced academicians and educators, ensures that every title balances conceptual rigor with age-appropriate presentation. High-quality printing, vibrant illustrations, and timely delivery define our commitment.

Syllabus-based content aligned to national standards
Activity-based learning for better retention
Affordable pricing for all school budgets
On-time delivery guaranteed, every academic year

"To empower students and support educators through affordable, well-structured educational resources that spark curiosity and build strong foundations."
